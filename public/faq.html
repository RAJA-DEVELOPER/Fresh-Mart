How can we help?

Find answers to common questions about orders, delivery, and more.

Delivery & Shipping

What are your delivery hours?
We deliver fresh groceries from 7:00 AM to 10:00 PM every day, including weekends and public holidays. Our delivery slots are divided into 3-hour windows for your convenience.
How much is the delivery fee?
Delivery is FREE for all orders above ₹499. For orders below this amount, a nominal delivery fee of ₹40 applies to cover logistics and quality packaging.

Orders & Payments

Can I cancel my order?
Orders can be cancelled free of charge before they are marked as "Packed." Once packing begins, cancellations may not be possible as fresh items are already being prepared.
What payment methods do you accept?
We currently accept Cash on Delivery (COD). We are working on integrating online payments via UPI, Credit/Debit cards, and Net Banking very soon!

Product Quality

What if I receive damaged or bad quality products?
Quality is our top priority. If any item doesn't meet your expectations, you can return it to the delivery partner at the time of delivery for an instant refund or replacement.

Still have questions?

We're here to help! Reach out to our customer support team for any assistance.
